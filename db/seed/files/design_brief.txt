Website Redesign - Design Brief
================================

Project Goals:
- Modernize visual design
- Improve mobile responsiveness
- Increase conversion rate by 25%
- Reduce page load time

Target Audience:
- Age: 25-45
- Tech-savvy professionals
- Mobile-first users

Key Pages:
1. Homepage - Hero section with CTA
2. About - Company story and team
3. Services - Product showcase
4. Contact - Form and map
5. Blog - Article listing

Design Direction:
- Clean, minimalist aesthetic
- Bold typography
- Generous whitespace
- High-quality imagery
- Consistent brand colors
